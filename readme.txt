== Description ==

[Synchrony (SYF)](https://www.synchrony.com/) is a premier consumer financial services company delivering customized financing programs and award-winning banking products. 
We’re the largest provider of private label credit cards in the United States (based on purchase volume and receivables) and offer a diverse range of credit products, 
including co-branded credit cards, promotional financing, installment lending and loyalty programs, as well as FDIC-insured savings products through Synchrony Bank. 
Our investments in technology across multiple platforms—in-store, online and mobile—allow us to engage consumers when and where they want. 
Our programs and tools strengthen the relationship between our business partners and their customers, driving growth and opportunity across the board.
Adding the Synchrony Extension to your WooCommerce payments page enables easy integration of our Synchrony solution, which offers consumers the opportunity to apply 
for revolving and installment financial products and to complete their purchase at checkout with a seamless apply and buy flow.  All authorizations, captures and refunds are processed through WooCommerce. 



== Installation ==

= Requirements =

To install Synchrony Payments, you need:

* WordPress Version 5.3 or newer (installed)
* WooCommerce Version 3.9 or newer (installed and activated)
* PHP Version 7.1 or newer
* Synchrony business account

= Instructions =

1. Log in to WordPress admin.
2. Go to **Plugins > Add New**.
3. Search for the **Synchrony Payments** plugin.
4. Click on **Install Now** and wait until the plugin is installed successfully.
5. You can activate the plugin immediately by clicking on **Activate** now on the success page. If you want to activate it later, you can do so via **Plugins > Installed Plugins**.

= Setup and Configuration =

Follow the steps below to configure the plugin:

1. After you have activated the Synchrony Payments Payments plugin, go to **WooCommerce  > Settings**.
2. Click the **Payments** tab.
3. The Payment methods list will include Synchrony options. Click on **Synchrony**.
4. Complete the configurations in Sychrony Settings tab. 
8. Click **Save changes**.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will your advertisement widget work with custom themes?

Custom themes using WooCommerce hooks will work.

If you are not using WooCommerce hooks, the advertising widget will need to invoke the short code available within the extension to function.

= How do I troubleshoot the plugin.

Review the logs under WooCommerce > Status > Logs.
Contact your account representative.

= Does the Synchrony plugin support partial refunds?

Yes

= Does the Synchrony plugin support authorize only?

Yes

= How can I settle orders from the admin?

Go to WooCommerce > Settings > Payments > Synchrony > Payment Action.

= Does the Synchrony plugin display a monthly breakdown?

Yes, please contact account representative to enable this feature.

= Does the Synchrony plugin uses external services?

Yes, plugin uses services hosted by Synchrony Bank (plugin developer) to perform business functionality. 
Services provide features like Payment Authorization, Payment Settlement, Payment Refund, Widget display, Custom rule engine for personalize offers etc. 
Services consumed are hosted in domains below domains api.syf.com , shop.mysynchrony.com, pdpone.syfpos.com,  api-stg.syf.com, ushop.mysynchrony.com, spdpone.syfpos.com owned & managed by Synchrony Bank.

= Does the Synchrony plugin support WooCommerce blocks?

After the release of version 8.3, the Cart and Checkout pages are set as the default experience. We provide support for these pages.
